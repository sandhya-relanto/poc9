# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## AI-Powered Sales Coaching & Simulation Platform (POC)

---

# 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

The product is an AI-powered sales coaching platform designed to help organizations improve sales representative performance through simulated conversations, structured training, and data-driven feedback.

It enables managers to create realistic customer personas and assign training scenarios, while allowing reps to practice, receive AI-driven evaluation, and continuously improve.

---

# 2. PRODUCT VISION

To build a scalable, intelligent coaching system that prepares sales representatives for real-world customer interactions through AI simulations and actionable insights.

---

# 3. TARGET USERS

### Primary Users

* Sales Representatives (learn, practice, improve)
* Sales Managers (assign, monitor, coach)

### Secondary Users

* Sales Leadership (analytics, decision-making)

---

# 4. CORE USER PROBLEMS

### Sales Reps

* Lack of real-world practice before live calls
* Limited personalized feedback
* Unclear understanding of strengths and weaknesses

### Managers

* Cannot coach all reps effectively at scale
* Lack of visibility into rep performance
* No structured training system

---

# 5. PRODUCT GOALS

* Enable realistic AI-driven sales simulations
* Provide structured and repeatable training workflows
* Deliver real-time performance evaluation
* Improve rep communication and objection handling
* Provide managers with actionable coaching insights

---

# 6. KEY FEATURES

---

## 6.1 AUTHENTICATION & USER MANAGEMENT

* Role-based login (Manager / Rep)
* Organization-based mapping
* Secure authentication

---

## 6.2 SCENARIO & AI PERSONA CREATION

Managers can:

* Create AI personas with:

  * name
  * personality traits
  * difficulty
  * scenario context
  * objection style
  * evaluation focus

* Edit/Delete scenarios

* View scenario details

---

## 6.3 AI CONVERSATION SYSTEM

* Chat-based interaction with AI personas
* Dynamic responses based on persona configuration
* Multi-turn conversations
* Context-aware responses

---

## 6.4 TRAINING ASSIGNMENT SYSTEM

Managers can:

* Assign training scenarios
* Set deadlines and priorities
* Track assignment status

Reps can:

* View assigned missions
* Start training sessions
* Track completion

---

## 6.5 PERFORMANCE EVALUATION

After each session:

* AI evaluates rep performance

* Generates scores across parameters:

  * confidence
  * communication
  * listening
  * objection handling

* Generates:

  * strategic summary
  * tactical successes
  * operational gaps

---

## 6.6 INTELLIGENCE REPORTS

* Full detailed performance reports
* Visual score breakdowns
* AI-generated insights
* PDF download support

---

## 6.7 COACHING SYSTEM

Managers can:

* Send coaching notes
* Track coaching history

Reps can:

* View feedback
* Acknowledge notes

---

## 6.8 ANALYTICS & DASHBOARDS

### Manager Dashboard

* Team performance overview
* Rep analytics
* Assignment tracking
* Risk indicators

### Rep Dashboard

* Active missions
* Performance status
* Skill analytics
* AI recommendations

---
